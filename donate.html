M-Pesa Instructions

To make a donation through M-Pesa, follow these steps:

  1. Go to your M-Pesa menu on your mobile phone.
  2. Select "Lipa na M-Pesa."
  3. Select "Pay Bill."
  4. Enter the Pay Bill number: 247247
  5. Account no / Equity till: 0759241152
  6. Enter your donation amount.
  7. Enter your M-Pesa PIN.
  8. Confirm the details and press "OK."
